Characters:

Action points:
At the start of each round a character receives action points based on his properties

Movement points:
At the start of each round a character receives movement points based on his properties

Health points:
Every characters health points are in the range of [0, 100]. When a character drops to 0 HP an exfiltration is initiated. Meaning his HP will be set to 1 and he will be placed onto a random bar seat.

Intelligence points:
Score representing how good of a spy a character is. IPs can be earned by discovering classified information by e.g. spying on other characters or picking safes.

Properties:
Every character has a set of properties affecting his stats. Refer to the property section for more details

Inventory:
A character can carry gadgets in his inventory. A cocktail is a special kind of gadget.

Chips:
Every character starts the game with 10 chips. He can use them to play roulette on roulette table fields
